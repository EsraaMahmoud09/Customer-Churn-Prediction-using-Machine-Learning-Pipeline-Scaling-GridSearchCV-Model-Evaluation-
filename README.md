# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis & Prediction

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

* Customers with **higher monthly charges** are more likely to churn.
* **Long-term customers (high tenure)** show significantly lower churn rates.
* Customers without **Tech Support or Online Security** tend to leave more frequently.
* **Contract type** plays a major role — month-to-month contracts have the highest churn.
* Payment method and billing type (paperless billing) also impact churn behavior.

💡 These insights can help businesses:

* Improve customer retention strategies
* Target high-risk customers
* Optimize pricing and service offerings
